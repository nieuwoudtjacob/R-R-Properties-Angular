Practical Workschool

SOUTH AFRICAN COUNCIL FOR THE PROPERTY VALUERS PROFESSION

HOSTING OF THE PRACTICAL WORKSCHOOL FROM 26 – 30 AUGUST 2024

VENUE:

Johannesburg Business School

69 Kingsway Ave,

Auckland Park,

Johannesburg,

2092


Registration is now open and it is the first post Covid 19 practical work school that will be hosted as normal a contact session.



Closing date for registration is 31 July 2024.


  1. Who may attend the Work School
    • Candidate Valuer (including a Candidate Single Residential Property Assessor – CSRPA) in his/her final year of study of an accredited valuation qualification and who will be graduating in 2024
    • A Candidate Valuer who has completed his/her valuation qualification and wishes to sit for the Council’s admission examination to become a professional
    • A Candidate Single Residential Property Assessor (CSRPA) who will attend fewer sessions of the work school and expected to take the test on the Time Value of Money (TVM)
    • A Professional Associated Valuer or Professional Valuer who is already registered with the Council and wants to refresh his/her valuation knowledge and earn CPD points
    • A Candidate Valuer or a Professional Associated Valuer in preparation for the Board examination which was previously referred to as the pre-exam session
    • A delegate attending the practical work school as a requirement for the Board examination who will obtain a Certificate of Attendance
    • Any unregistered person ie a person not registered with the Council.

Please note: It is compulsory that a delegate must attend the practical work school as a requirement to sit for the Board examination (to become professionals).
