Restriction Codes

Areas of work

PROFESSIONAL ASSOCIATED VALUERS REGISTERED IN TERMS OF SECTION 20(2)(a) OF THE PROPERTY VALUERS PROFESSION ACT, 2000 (ACT NO. 47 OF 2000)

Where the registration number is followed by a letter or letters, that person is, in terms of his/her registration, entitled to practice property valuation in a specific field only. A list of the letters and the corresponding field follows.

Some professional associated valuers choose to limit their activities to a specific field and those persons are often the experts in their particular fields.

List of areas of work (Restriction)

  • Assessment rates & Endowment for a Local Authority
  • Mortgage Bonds
  • Mortgage Bonds on Single Residential Properties (Single Dwellings, Erven for Single Dwellings, Individual Residential Sectional Title Units and Single Dwellings on Agricultural Land Single Residential Properties (Single Dwellings, Erven for Single Dwellings and Individual Residential Sectional Title Units)
  • Agricultural Properties

  • Local Authority & Single Residential
