Learnership

Learnership Requirements:

All unemployed candidates/ valuers should send an email to kate@sacpvp.co.za, with the following information:

  1. Full names and Surname
  2. Contact Number(s)
  3. E-mail address.

The Council will submit your name to any company looking for candidates or valuers. After your name has been submitted to a company, they will communicate directly with you.

Thank you

SACPVP
